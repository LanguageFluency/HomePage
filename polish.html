Polish To Get You Started!

Polish is an official language of only Poland, which makes it unique. It is written standard is the Polish alphabet, which has nine additions to the letters of the basic Latin script. It is also 10th most difficult language to learn in the world.
Interestingly, if you learn Polish you will also be able to understand Czech and Slovak as they are closely related, even compared with other Slavic languages. However, there are also a number of words in these two languages that have the
same pronunciation as a Polish word but an entirely different meaning!
