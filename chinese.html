Chinese to get you started

With approximately 1.2 billion speakers around the world, Mandarin is the most commonly used Chinese dialect and the official language of the People’s Republic of China. Mandarin is written using characters, however a romanized phonetic spelling system known as Pinyin was created in the 1950s as a way of more easily teaching a single standard of pronunciation. This is also extremely useful for foreigners trying to learn the language, as it shows the tones and a (somewhat) recognisable combination of letters!
