Spanish To Get You Started!

Spanish is the second most spoken language in the world after Mandarin. It is a romance language with Latin origins, but has also been influenced heavily by Arabic. Today, across the world, it is spoken by 414 million people, and is the official language of 21 countries. This includes Spain and most of Latin and Central America. Given this, it is unsurprising that it has many regional variations and there is no “standard spanish”. The following are some of the phrases commonly used in Argentina.
